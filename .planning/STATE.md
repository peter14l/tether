## Context

**Tether** is an emotional-safety-first social connection app for close relationships (friends, couples, families). Not a social media platform. No public feeds, no follower counts, no algorithms.

- **Frontend:** Flutter (iOS & Android)
- **Backend:** Supabase
- **Architecture:** Clean Architecture
- **Features:** 75+ planned
