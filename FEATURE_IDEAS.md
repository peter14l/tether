# Oasis - Feature Ideas for Emotional Safety & Peace

## Core Vision
> "A safe space away from the busy noise of the world. Users should feel safe, emotionally secure, and comfortable. Something they can ease and relax in and connect with friends."

---

## General Wellness Features (1-25)

### 1. Mood Rooms 🌤️
A dedicated space where users can set their current emotional state (e.g., "Need Quiet," "Feeling anxious," "Just want to chat"). Friends can see this and respond accordingly — no pressure to engage if someone's not in the right headspace.

### 2. Quiet Hours / Wind Down Mode 🌙
Scheduled blocks where the app goes silent — no notifications, no calls, no DMs. Users can set "Wind Down" periods (like 9pm–7am) and the app gently fades to a dimmed UI, blocking itself after a set time.

### 3. Gratitude Journal 🙏
A private, encrypted space to log daily gratitudes. Users can optionally share these with their Circles. Simple prompts: "What made you smile today?"

### 4. Voice Notes Only 🎙️
A "Slow Chat" mode where users can only send voice notes — no text, no images, no pressure. Just talking. More personal than text, less intimidating than a call.

### 5. Shared Playlists / Ambient Sounds 🎵
Curated ambient sound mixes users can share with friends — rain sounds, lo-fi, white noise. Great for studying together or just existing in the same digital space.

### 6. Memories Lane 📸
A chronological walk through shared moments with friends — photos, voice notes, milestones — organized by month. No likes, no comments. Just "remember when?"

### 7. Check-In System 🤝
A daily "I'm okay" one-tap check-in friends can see. For tight circles, it's reassurance. No tracking, just peace of mind. Can trigger gentle follow-ups if someone misses their check-in.

### 8. No-Likes, No-Counts 👻
Hide engagement numbers from posts within Circles. People engage because they want to, not because of social proof.

### 9. Breathing Exercises On Open 🌬️
On app launch, a 3-second calming animation with a deep breath. It grounds users before they dive in.

### 10. Safe Space Lock 🔐
A second-level security to hide specific Circles, DMs, or Vault items. For users who share devices or need extra emotional privacy.

### 11. Temperature Check 🌡️
A daily poll in each Circle: "How are you feeling today?" with emoji options. No comments, just a visible dot next to each friend showing their state.

### 12. Soft Block 🌫️
Block someone without notifying them. They simply stop seeing your content. No confrontation, no drama.

### 13. Comfort Radius 🎯
Choose how far out content goes: "Inner Circle" (1-5), "Close Circle" (10), "All Friends." Users control intimacy, not algorithms.

### 14. Gentle Reactions 💫
Replace likes with: "Warm," "Comforting," "I see you," "Sending strength." No numbers, no leaderboards.

### 15. Digital Hug 🤗
Send a notification that appears as a gentle "bump" — no text, no sound, just a warm pulse. They can send one back or just sit with it.

### 16. Breathing Room 🌬️
A panic/take-a-moment screen — slow 4-7-8 breathing animation with optional ambient sound. For anxiety, overwhelm, or just needing a pause.

### 17. Sunset Mode 🌅
Auto-activate after sunset: warmer colors, dimmed UI, no bright whites. The app winds down with the day. Respects circadian rhythms.

### 18. No-Algorithm Promise 📜
A visible toggle: "Show me everything, in order." No ranking, no optimization. Users always know the feed is chronological.

### 19. Letter Mode ✉️
Write a "letter" to a friend — longer form, thoughtful. Delivered at a chosen time (tomorrow morning, next weekend). Anti-instant, anti-impulsive.

### 20. Reflection Wall 🧱
A private, encrypted space to write unfiltered thoughts — vent, process, celebrate. Not shared unless the user chooses. Never seen by anyone.

### 21. Presence, Not Pressure 👀
See friends are "Online" but labeled as "Quiet" — meaning they're online but not looking to chat. No "last seen" anxiety.

### 22. Kindness Streaks 🌻
Track small acts of kindness within your Circle — "sent a voice note," "checked in," "shared something." No gamification pressure.

### 23. Shared Calendar 📆
A shared calendar where friends mark important dates — not just birthdays, but "anniversary of our first message," "the day we met."

### 24. No-Notification Windows 🤫
Set recurring quiet blocks: "No notifications between 9pm–7am" or "No calls after 10pm." The app respects this hard.

### 25. Slow Photo 🖼️
Photos load with a calm, slow fade-in animation. No jarring pop-in. The entire UX should feel like breathing.

## Couples Features (26-35)

### 26. Our Bubble 💕
A private, encrypted space just for the two of you. No one else sees it. Shared calendar, shared memories, shared everything. Your own digital home.

### 27. Love Languages Tracker 💬
Track how you and your partner show love: words, time, gifts, acts, touch. Get gentle suggestions on what your partner needs more of.

### 28. Relationship Milestones 🎂
Automatically track moments: "First message," "6 months," "First voice call," "First photo shared." A private timeline of your story together.

### 29. Good Morning / Good Night 💌
Scheduled daily messages that arrive automatically. Wake up to a sweet note, fall asleep to a goodnight message. No forgetting, no excuses.

### 30. Together Mode 📱
When both users open the app at the same time, a special "together" UI appears — share music, ambient sounds, or just exist in the same digital space.

### 31. Heartbeat Check 💓
A quick "I'm thinking of you" pulse that shows on your partner's lock screen. No notification sound, just a visual warmth.

### 32. Date Planner 📅
A shared space to plan dates — ideas, reservations, reminders. Not just "what shall we do?" but "let's plan something special."

### 33. Private Gallery 🖼️
A shared photo space only the two of you can see. No cloud, no public albums. Just your moments together, encrypted.

### 34. Promise Rings (Digital) 💍
Digital commitment markers — "I choose you today" with a timestamp. Small rituals that mean something.

### 35. Space to Breathe 🌿
When things are tough, a "pause" button that says "I need space, but I love you." No argument, no stress. Just a quiet signal.

## Family Features (36-45)

### 36. Family Circle 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
A dedicated Circle for family with special features: automatic location sharing (optional), safety check-ins, and family-only posts.

### 37. Safety Check 🛡️
Parents can set "Are you safe?" triggers that family members respond to. If someone doesn't respond within X minutes, designated contacts get alerted.

### 38. Kid Mode 🧸
A restricted view for children under 13 (or custom age). Parents control what content appears. No public internet, just family.

### 39. Grandparent Easy View 👴👵
A simplified UI option with larger text, fewer options, and one-tap video calling. Designed for elderly family members.

### 40. Family Feed 📻
A private, chronological feed only family sees. No algorithms, no ads. Just updates from the ones you love.

### 41. Location Family 🌎
Optional live location sharing within the family Circle. "Mom's at home," "Dad's at work." Privacy-respecting, always-on permission.

### 42. Reminder Board 📝
A shared to-do board: "Pick up milk," "Call grandma," "Doctor appointment." The family that reminds together.

### 43. Heritage Corner 🌍
A space to share family history — old photos, stories, traditions. Connect generations through shared memory.

### 44. Emergency SOS 🚨
One-tap alert to all family members with your location. For when something's wrong and you need help fast.

### 45. Generational Memories 📂
Elder family members can upload old photos with context. Younger members can ask questions, add comments. A living family archive.

## Additional Couples Features (46-55)

### 46. Our Song 🎵
A song you both designate as "yours." Whenever one of you opens a chat, the song plays softly. You can share songs back and forth as messages.

### 47. Favor Coupons 🎟️
Generate "I owe you" coupons for your partner: "One back rub," "Pick dinner," "No arguments today." Redeemable anytime.

### 48. Sleep Together 🌙
When both partners are online at bedtime, the app enters "Sleep Together" mode — ambient rain, white noise, or a shared playlist playing. One person controls it.

### 49. Mood Sync 🌡️
See your partner's Mood Room status on your lock screen. Not intrusive, just a quiet awareness of how they're doing.

### 50. Private Jokes Vault 😂
A shared space for screenshots, memes, inside jokes only you two understand. No one else can see it.

### 51. Future Letter 💌
Write a letter to be delivered in the future — "Open this on our anniversary." A digital time capsule for your relationship.

### 52. Relationship Advice Library 📚
Curated, gentle relationship advice articles. No toxic productivity. Just healthy, calm guidance.

### 53. Distance Mode 🌍
For long-distance couples: a dedicated mode with more thoughtful features — longer voice notes, scheduled calls, shared music, "I miss you" triggers.

### 54. Little Surprises 🎁
One partner can schedule a "surprise" that pops up on the other's screen at a random time. No advance warning. Just a small delight.

### 55. Anniversary Countdown 🎉
A countdown widget to your next anniversary, milestone, or special date. Shared between both partners.

## Additional Family Features (56-65)

### 56. Chore Chart 📋
A shared family chore list with assignments. Kids earn points, parents get help. No nagging needed.

### 57. Prayer / Meditation Time 🙏
Set a daily family prayer or meditation time. When it's time, the app sends a gentle reminder to all family members who opted in.

### 58. Bedtime Stories 📖
Record voice-over of parents reading stories. Kids can play them back anytime. Grandparents can record too.

### 59. Family Rituals 🎎
Schedule weekly family rituals: "Sunday dinner call," "Friday movie night." The app reminds and connects everyone.

### 60. Homework Help Network 📚
Family members can post "I need help with X" and whoever's available can jump in. Cousins, older siblings, parents.

### 61. Family Pet Corner 🐾
A dedicated photo feed for pet updates. Grandma's cat, Uncle's dog, everyone's favorite animal updates.

### 62. In-Law Circle 👥
A special Circle type for in-laws only. Boundaries built in. Less pressure, more respect.

### 63. Sibling Bond Tracker 👫
For siblings: track shared moments, send "thinking of you" notes, coordinate secretly for birthday surprises.

### 64. Family Reunion Planner 🎊
A dedicated space to plan family reunions — polling dates, collecting contributions, sharing photos after.

### 65. Emergency Family Contact 🚨
One-tap alert to all family members with location. Designed for real emergencies, not just "call me."

## Even More Ideas (66+)

### 66. Anonymous Venting 🤫
Share a feeling anonymously within a Circle. Friends can respond with comfort, but don't know who sent it.

### 67. One-Way Post 📤
Send a post that disappears after being seen. For "I wanted to tell you this but couldn't say it."

### 68. Soft Delete 🗑️
Posts you delete go to a "soft trash" for 30 days before permanent deletion. No accidental loss.

### 69. Accessibility Mode ♿
Full screen reader support, high contrast, larger fonts. The app should be for everyone.

### 70. Mood-Adaptive UI 🎨
The app's colors subtly shift based on the user's Mood Room status. Calm = cool blues. Happy = warm yellows.

### 71. Gentle Nudge 🌱
A notification that says "Your friend [Name] posted something" — without showing what. They can choose to check it out or not. No algorithmic push.

### 72. Once Upon a Time 📖
A "this day last year" feature. "You posted this this day last year with [friend]." Warm nostalgia, no pressure.

### 73. Whisper Mode 🤫
A mode where all text becomes whispers (smaller, softer font). Everything feels calmer. Use it in the evenings.

### 74. Mirror Mode 🪞
A front-camera "check in" — users can see their own face while recording a message. Some people need to see themselves to feel comfortable.

### 75. Gentle Gamification 🌱
Earn quiet badges for things like "Checked in 7 days in a row," "Sent 3 voice notes this week." No leaderboards, no competition. Just personal milestones.
